Controlled Substances & Prescription

Legal note: Psilocybin and MDMA are Schedule I in the US. Hawaii has no state-level decriminalization. Know your legal context. Propranolol requires a physician prescription. MDMA testing with all three reagents is mandatory for every batch.
Status Item Phase / Protocol Use Notes & Specs
Psilocybin (dried mushrooms)
Weighed, sourced responsibly
 Phase 0–5 Session 0: 1.5–2g. Phases 1–2 orientation: 1.5–2g. Phase 2 Session 1: 2.5g. Phase 3: 2.5–3g. Phase 4: 2.5–3.5g. Phase 5: 2–3g q2–3 months. Microdose: 0.1–0.15g Mon–Thu. Weigh every dose on 0.001g scale.
MDMA
Tested with all 3 reagents every batch
 Phase 3–4 Session 1 (Phase 3): 75mg. Session 2 (Phase 4): 75–100mg. Maximum 2 sessions in 12 months. Minimum 6-week interval. Test with Marquis + Simon's + fentanyl strips — every batch, no exceptions.
Propranolol
10–20mg, prescription required
 Phase 3–5 Beta-blocker for memory reconsolidation. 10–20mg taken 60–90 min before CC session. Requires physician prescription. Not for asthma or low baseline HR. Not within 48 hrs of macrodose or MDMA.

Food-based protocol components — not supplements, but each has a specific mechanistic role. Keep stocked as part of the regular shop.

Kukicha or Sencha Green Tea
 Daily, afternoon (2–3 hrs before sleep) Lower caffeine than matcha. EGCG, trace L-theanine. Wind-down ritual quality. Space 1 hr from iron-rich meals.
Wheat Germ
 1–2 tbsp daily in smoothie or oats Highest spermidine concentration per gram of any food. Triggers autophagy — cellular clearing between plasticity events. Shelf-stable in fridge after opening.
Capers
 Daily — add to anything Highest quercetin food source (~20mg/tbsp). Clears senescent cells, reduces inflammation. Senolytic. Phase 5 emphasizes deliberate daily inclusion, but useful throughout.
Apple Cider Vinegar
 1 tbsp before meals AMPK activation, blood sugar management. Dilute in water. Same AMPK pathway as berberine but food-based, safe daily.
Ground Flaxseed
 Daily in smoothies or oats ALA (omega-3 precursor), lignans, polyphenols. Keep refrigerated after grinding/opening. Grind fresh when possible for freshness.
Mustard Powder
 Add when cooking brassicas Restores sulforaphane production in cooked broccoli/cauliflower (myrosinase enzyme replacement). Also: chop brassicas 40 min before cooking to pre-activate sulforaphane.
Dark Chocolate 85%+
 Daily Theobromine, flavanols. Anti-inflammatory. Protocol food, not indulgence framing. 85% minimum cocoa content.
